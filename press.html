Biography

Moon Mountain String Band is a seven-piece outfit grown in the forests of western Oregon. With a blend of traditional bluegrass roots and newgrass flair, the band captivates audiences across the region with high lonesome melodies, rich vocal harmonies, and an infectious groove that will get you dancing.

Featuring a dynamic lineup including banjo, mandolin, fiddle, dobro, twin guitars and acoustic bass, Moon Mountain crafts a sound that resonates with both seasoned bluegrass fans and newcomers.
