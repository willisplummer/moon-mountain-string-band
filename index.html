About the Band

Moon Mountain is a seven-piece band grown in the forests of western Oregon. With a blend of traditional bluegrass roots and newgrass flair, our band captivates audiences across the state with rich harmonies and technical musicianship. Featuring a dynamic lineup including banjo, mandolin, fiddle, dobro, and guitar, we craft a sound that resonates with both seasoned bluegrass fans and newcomers alike.
